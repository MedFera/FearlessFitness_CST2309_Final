Training Cancellation Policy:

Please respect the community and time of our trainers. You must cancel through our website. If you are going to miss a class or training session you booked, you must do so in the following timeframes:

Group Training and Yoga: 2 hours before the start of the class.

Semi-Private Training: 12 hours before the start time of the training.

Personal Training: Must contact your coach 24 hours before the start time of the training.

If you do not cancel within the above timeframe, you will forfeit the credit for such training.

Freeze Policy:

We get that things come up! We offer our members the option to freeze their membership up to two times per calendar year.

During a freeze, you will not incur membership fees, nor will you incur any freeze or unfreeze fees. You must freeze your membership for a minimum of 30 days, but in no event for more than 90 days. After 90 days, your membership will unfreeze and your monthly membership fees shall resume, unless you cancel your membership in accordance with our cancellation policy.

Cancellation Policy:

You can cancel your membership at any time, for any reason. If you cancel your membership before your next billing cycle, you will not be charged for that next cycle. However, if you fail to notify us of desire to cancel before you are billed for a cycle, your membership will remain in effect until the end of the current billing cycle and we will not issue any refunds.

You can submit notice for cancellation by emailing info@brooklynathleticclub.com.

Referral Policy:

To thank you for your loyalty, when you refer a friend to Fearless and they sign up for a membership you will both receive $50 in account credit. Such credit cannot be redeemed for cash.
